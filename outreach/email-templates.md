## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Email Collection
- Scrape contractor email addresses from their websites (most have contact forms or email on their site)
- Start with contractors who have high ratings (4.5+) and working websites
- Prioritize cities with the most search volume

### Phase 2: Batch Send
- Send Template 1 to 20-30 contractors per day (avoid spam filters)
- Personalize the city and business name
- Track opens and replies

### Phase 3: Follow-up
- Send Template 2 to non-responders after 7 days
- Send Template 3 to contractors who claim their listing

### Phase 4: Measure
- Track claim rate (goal: 5-10%)
- Track backlink rate (contractors linking to their profile)
- Monitor organic search traffic growth

### Expected Results
- 1,000 contractors × 5% claim rate = 50 claimed profiles
- 50 claimed × 20% link back = 10 backlinks (these are gold for SEO)
- Even without backlinks, claimed profiles = more accurate data = better user experience
